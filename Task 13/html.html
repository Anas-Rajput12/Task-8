Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to your common questions

What is the duration of the internship?

The internship typically lasts for 4 to 6 weeks depending on the program schedule.

Is the internship paid?

No, the internship is unpaid but you will receive a certificate upon successful completion.

Can I work remotely?

Yes, the internship is fully remote and can be completed from anywhere with internet access.

Will I get a certificate?

Yes, you will receive a certificate after completing all tasks and evaluations.

How do I submit my tasks?

You can submit your tasks via GitHub repository link or through a zipped folder.
