Frequently Asked Questions

What is the duration of the internship? +
The internship lasts for 8 weeks and includes both training and project work.

This is an unpaid internship, but a certificate is provided upon successful completion.
Can students from any field apply? +
Yes, students from all academic backgrounds are encouraged to apply.
Will I receive a certificate? +
Yes, a digital certificate will be issued after all tasks are completed.
Do I need prior experience? +
No prior experience is required, but a basic understanding of web development is helpful.
How do I apply for the internship? +
You can apply via our official website through the application form provided.
